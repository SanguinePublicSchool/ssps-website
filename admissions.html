Admissions Open for Academic Year 2026-27

Registration forms are available online and at the school administration office. Limited seats available for Kindergarten to Grade 10.

Age Eligibility

Please review the age requirements below before filling out the registration form:

Grade Minimum Age
Nursery 3 Years
LKG 4 Years
UKG 5 Years
Grade 1 6 Years

Admission Process

  • Step 1: Obtain the application form from the school office or portal.
  • Step 2: Submit the completed form along with required documents.
  • Step 3: Attend the interactive session/interaction test.
  • Step 4: Secure admission by paying the term fees within the due date.
