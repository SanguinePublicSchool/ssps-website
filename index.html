About Our School

Providing quality education and fostering excellence since 2010. Our school focuses on holistic development, modern infrastructure, and empowering students for a bright future.

At Sanguine Public School, we blend strong academic foundations with extracurricular opportunity. Students benefit from a caring environment, leadership programs, cultural activities, and community service that build confidence and respect.

We are committed to nurturing every child with personalized attention, ethical values, and a spirit of innovation. Our classrooms are safe, inclusive, and designed to inspire curiosity and creativity.

  • Modern Science & Computer Labs
  • Spacious Playground & Sports Facilities
  • Smart Classrooms for Interactive Learning
  • Strong Focus on Discipline and Character
  • Regular Cultural, Sports, and STEM Events
